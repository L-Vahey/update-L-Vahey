Project:
Link and Learn

Year:
2017

Link and learn is a learning festival consisting of talks, workshops and sign ups, that celebrates learning for people over 55 years old in Ireland. We designed a buddy system to tackle the emotional barriers that prevent the older generation from upskilling or starting a new course. This was aided by friendly language and playful visuals.
