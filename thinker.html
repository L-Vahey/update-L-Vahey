ISTD

Project:
Voluntourism

Brief:
Protest

Year:
2019

This publication aims to inform college students about damage caused by taking part in short-term, volunteer work in developing countries. It is a tabloid style, ephemeral piece designed to be given out at college events. Satirical interruptions and social media commentary appear throughout as a way of highlighting how well intentioned volunteers are contributing to the commodification of vulnerable children.
